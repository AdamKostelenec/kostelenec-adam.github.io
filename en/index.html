02 — Profile

About Me

My name is Adam Kostelenec and I am a student at the Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at VŠB – Technical University of Ostrava, where I study Information Technology.

For several years, I have been focused on software and web development — from small automation tools to full-scale web applications.

Currently, I work as a programmer at NANO Systems s.r.o., where I am responsible for the complete development of applications for the Shoptet e-commerce platform in C#:

  • data integration using REST API,
  • processing and transformation of XML feeds,
  • design and implementation of backend services,
  • e-shop template customization (HTML, CSS, JavaScript),
  • development of internal process automation tools.

In the past, I also worked at Gamisport – Jeans s.r.o., where I took over the management of the company’s e-shop division and gained experience in technical e-shop management, marketing, and business communication.

Recently, I have been strongly focused on building modern websites — clean, responsive, and optimized for speed and usability. I enjoy combining technical precision with visual design to create websites that are not only functional but also pleasant to use.

I believe a good developer should understand not only code, but also the people who use their work. That’s why I try to think about projects in a broader context — from user experience and performance to maintainability and scalability.

At the moment, I am learning more advanced web technologies, such as JavaScript frameworks (React, Vue), while continuing to improve my backend knowledge in .NET.

Summary: I combine analytical thinking with attention to detail, I enjoy explaining complex things simply, and I get the most satisfaction when my solutions genuinely make someone’s work easier.
